Dragonball Z: The Ultimate Story

This site is designed to give someone with no knowedlge of Dragon Ball Z, an insite to one of the greatest stories ever told!

Goku

Goku, the main protagonist of Dragonball Z, is a Saiyan warrior sent on a mission as a baby to take over planet Earth. As a child, Goku loses his memory and grows up with a gentle, kind heart under the care of his grandfather, Gohan. Goku learns martial arts and becomes one of the best fighters in the world, always looking for other opponents to help him test his limits. Throughout Dragonball Z, Goku gets into dangerous encounters with powerful enemies to protect his loved ones and planet Earth. Known for his strong determination, boundless optimism, and love for battle, Goku often pushes himself beyond his limits, achieving legendary transformations. His strength, heart, and loyalty make him a beloved hero.

Vegata

Vegeta, the Prince of all Saiyans, is one of the main characters in Dragonball Z. Known for his pride and fierce rivalry with Goku, Vegata has a relentless drive to be the strongest just like Goku. Despite starting as a villain in the series, his character delopment significally improves over time, forming alliances and even friendships with the Z Fighters to protect planet Earth from various threats.

Gohan

Gohan, the eldest son of Goku and Chi-Chi in Dragon Ball Z, is a character who starts off as a shy, gentle kid with immense untapped power. Initially uninterested in fighting, he trains under Piccolo after the death of Goku during the Saiyan Saga, developing both his combat skills and mental resilience. Gohan’s character arc focuses on his struggle between his desire for a peaceful life and his duty to protect others. His greatest moments include his transformation into Super Saiyan 2 during the Cell Games, where he ultimately defeats Cell and becomes Earth’s strongest warrior for a time. As he grows older, Gohan steps back from fighting to pursue academics, though he remains willing to step up when Earth is in danger. His internal conflict between his peaceful nature and his powerful Saiyan heritage makes him one of the series’ most complex and beloved characters.

Piccolo

Piccolo, originally introduced as a formidable adversary in Dragon Ball, undergoes significant development throughout Dragon Ball Z. As the reincarnation of King Piccolo, he initially wanted to conquer Earth and defeat Goku. However, after training Goku’s son, Gohan, Piccolo’s character transforms, showcasing his capacity for compassion and selflessness. He becomes a crucial ally to the Z Fighters, often acting as a mentor and protector, especially to Gohan. Piccolo’s journey from villain to hero highlights themes of redemption and personal growth, making him a beloved and complex character in the series.
